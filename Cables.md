-----------|---------|--------|--------------------|----------------|

100BASE-T  | 802.3u  | 100Mbps| Fast-Ethernet      | 100m
1000BASE-T | 802.3ab | 1Gbps  | Gigabit Ethernet   | 100m
10GBASE-T  | 802.3an | 10Gbps | 10Gigabit Ethernet | 100m
